01 · Why Moduł System

Advanced building physics & insulation

We use certified sandwich panels with a hard mineral wool or rigid PIR polyurethane foam core (e.g. 120 mm) and proprietary sandwich structures. This technology guarantees excellent thermal stability for sensitive ICT equipment, elimination of condensation, and compliance with strict fire protection requirements.

Sandwich-panel technical shelters maintain stable climate conditions for sensitive electronic equipment — even in extreme environments including Alaska, Africa and the Middle East.

  • Fire resistance rated EI30 / EI60 available
  • PIR polyurethane foam or mineral wool core
  • 120 mm panel thickness for extreme climates
  • Zero condensation risk with continuous insulation
  • Certified for operation from −50°C to +55°C ambient

Technical shelter vs. adapted shipping container

A purpose-built technical shelter is a fundamentally different engineering solution

Feature Adapted shipping container Moduł System Technical Shelter
Dimensions Fixed — width limited to approx. 2.4 m. No flexibility for equipment layout. Full flexibility — dimensions dictated by equipment size and functional layout. No standard limits.
Thermal insulation Internal insulation required — high risk of concealed thermal bridges and condensation. Integrated sandwich panel providing continuous insulation and zero dew-point risk.
Technology openings Cutting openings in finished sheet permanently weakens structural load capacity. Openings, grilles and penetrations designed and reinforced during frame welding.
Floor system Standard plywood — difficult to replace with engineered cable management floor systems. Engineered technical floor designed for high loads (RACK cabinets, batteries, switchgear).
Systems integration Requires full fit-out on site, without factory quality control conditions. Full FAT — HVAC, electrical, cable management installed and tested in factory before delivery.
